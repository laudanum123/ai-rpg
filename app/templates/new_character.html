Fighter

Masters of martial combat, fighters are skilled with weapons and armor. They are tough and can withstand significant damage.

  • +2 Strength, +1 Constitution
  • Extra health points
  • Starts with a longsword and shield
  • Special ability: Second Wind

Wizard

Scholars of arcane magic, wizards can cast powerful spells to defeat enemies and solve problems.

  • +2 Intelligence, +1 Wisdom
  • Starts with a staff and spellbook
  • Can learn and cast various spells
  • Special ability: Arcane Recovery

Rogue

Masters of stealth and precision, rogues excel at finding and disabling traps, picking locks, and striking vulnerable targets.

  • +2 Dexterity, +1 Charisma
  • Starts with a dagger and thieves' tools
  • Skilled at stealth and deception
  • Special ability: Sneak Attack
